Services

Candles

Premium beeswax and soy wax candles are crafted by blending the finest beeswax, known for its natural scent and long burn time, with eco-friendly soy wax from renewable soybean crops. The combined waxes are melted, poured into molds with wicks, and cooled. These candles offer a clean, even burn, and a warm, rich aroma.

Soaps

Experience the pinnacle of self-care with our artisanal cold process soap, crafted from a blend of luxurious cocoa, shea, and mango butters with premium oils. Skillfully combined with a lye solution for saponification, these lavish soap bars are poured into molds and cured. The result is a moisturizing soap that gently cleanses and pampers your skin, leaving it soft and rejuvenated. Indulge in this sumptuous treat and elevate your self-care routine.

About

Learn about our history.

  • 2020-2021

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our journey began amidst the uncertainty of the COVID pandemic, when a passion for artisanal self-care products sparked a creative endeavor. With dedication and curiosity, we experimented tirelessly, testing various formulas in pursuit of the perfect bar of soap. As we refined our craftsmanship, our unique blend of nourishing ingredients emerged, culminating in an exceptional soap that not only cleanses but also pampers the skin. Inspired by our success, we expanded our offerings, creating a thriving business that celebrates the art of self-care and the transformative power of handcrafted products.

  • March 2023

    An Enterprise is Born

    After two years of honing our skills and perfecting our craft, we felt a calling to share our passion for self-care with the world. Driven by the desire to elevate daily rituals, we launched our collection of artisanal soaps, candles, and other thoughtfully curated items, each boasting a unique touch. Our dedication to quality and authenticity is evident in every individually crafted product, designed to indulge the senses and create a haven of relaxation. The distinctiveness of each item adds a personal charm, making your self-care experience truly one-of-a-kind. Now, as we embark on this exciting journey, we invite you to discover the transformative power of our handcrafted creations, and join us in celebrating the art of self-care, one luxurious moment at a time.
